Simple & Practical Desktop Software

SceneOne - Familiar yet full of surprises.

Native macOS Experience

All the shortcuts you know and love - Quick Look with spacebar, undo and redo, find and replace - everything works just as you expect. Work with multiple windows, freely copy and paste between documents, and enjoy automatic local backups - just like your favorite professional software.

Smart Image Import

Drag and drop images or videos, and SceneOne instantly adjusts them to 16:9 ratio. We've specially optimized our cropping algorithm for Cardrage images, ensuring clean and crisp document layouts.

Say Goodbye to Repetitive Input

Shooting dates, times, locations - all these repetitive details can be batch-filled and modified in one click.
Remember the Terminal's history feature? Just like that, press up/down to recall previous inputs.
SceneOne comes with presets for cameras, focal lengths, and angles - type two or three characters for instant matches. It remembers your common inputs, and you can save custom entries to build your perfect shortcuts.

Effortless Schedule Changes

Need to adjust your shooting schedule? SceneOne makes it simple.
Moving afternoon shots to the morning? No sweat. The "Advanced Scheduling" feature maintains your shooting cycles and sequences while recalculating new shooting times.
What if there's already a morning shoot planned? The "Smart Time Avoidance" feature helps resolve conflicts by automatically adjusting overlapping shots, keeping your schedule perfectly organized.

Export Your Way

SceneOne comes with elegant PDF presets - portrait, landscape, with your specified information - all styles are covered without the formatting headaches.
Need to continue editing in other software? No problem. Export to docx, xlsx, or template-formatted pptx files. Use SceneOne for shot management, then seamlessly continue your work in other apps.

More than You'd Expect

SceneOne is more than just shot management software. It includes essential tools for image cropping, annotation, video trimming, call sheet generation, and presentation mode. All the most practical features are integrated into one place, letting you focus on what matters.
