SceneOne

Planning has never been this effortless!

Why do we need this?

SceneOne® is a shooting plan app for small studios and independent photographers. It avoids complex forms and excessive details, focusing on clear plans and effective communication..

Storyboard

A simple yet highly efficient Storyboard.

Rundown

With an intelligent Rundown that allows batch time adjustments.

Callsheet

A Callsheet that quickly loads and applies templates for easy filling.

Presentation

Easily present your shooting plan to clients or colleagues through a Slideshow.

Export

Export PDFs in various styles or use long images to wake up your colleagues.

Mobile

Browse shooting plans anytime, anywhere on your mobile device, and mark filming progress.

A more RESPONSIVE creation process

Focus more on thinking, not formatting.

Anytime

Edit your shooting plans on desktop. Using various forms such as images, videos, and notes. You can batch modify shooting times, delay or advance schedules, making complex or tight time coordination simpler. Multiple output formats are available for clients, shooting partners, and personal use.

Know more

Anywhere

Browse your shooting plans on iOS, quickly marking completed, delayed, and abandoned tasks, so you can stay focused and avoid confusion. The Quicklog feature allows the script supervisor to easily input shot suffix numbers to log file names, speeding up your post-production workflow.
