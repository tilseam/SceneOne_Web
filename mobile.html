Your Intuitive Shot Planning Companion

Think Sticky Note, Not Spreadsheet

Paper That Plays Video

On set, we usually print and carry a shooting schedule - portable but easily damaged or stained. Moreover, in the analog world, paper can only show images, not play videos.
Choose SceneOne to bring reference videos to the set, making communication crystal clear - whether it's about camera movements, angles, or creative ideas.

Complete Shots With a Swipe

A shooting schedule shouldn't be a cramped spreadsheet - it should be a visual gallery. We keep the essential information visible and neatly tuck away the rest, making your shot list as clean as Twitter.
Finished a shot? Instead of checking boxes on paper, simply swipe right to mark it complete. Swipe left to mark it as "postponed", and our notification dots ensure you never miss a shot.

Effortless Script Supervision

Recording video and audio filenames is tedious for script supervisors, yet these filenames often follow patterns after the first input. SceneOne's professional filename prediction algorithm works with various recording devices. Just input the changing numbers, and let it auto-complete the full filename.

* SceneOne QuickLog is available as an in-app purchase on iOS devices.
* Not compatible with complex filenames (e.g., RED cameras).

Share With Everyone

SceneOne mobile app works on both iOS and Android, allowing every crew member instant access. Feel confident integrating SceneOne into your workflow.

* Android version is currently in beta, offering core features only.
